About Us

In 2018, Julian Kage, CEO and Founder of Exactics, invented and patented QuickLyme, the first 15-minute rapid test for Lyme disease. He secured a second patent for a versatile core test design adaptable to over 200 arthropod-borne diseases, including Malaria, Zika, and Dengue Fever. This novel design enables easy antibody swaps, allowing for customizable tests across various diseases while keeping minimizing manufacturing costs.

QuickLyme®

Julian Kage, CEO of Exactics, developed QuickLyme, a rapid test that detects Lyme bacteria in ticks within 15 minutes and secured a patent with support from the Ford Foundation. Julian leads Exactics alongside Zach Sarmoen (CFO), Dylan Murray (COO), Sean Greeby (CSO), and Max Almeida (CTO). Our advisory board includes experts from Harvard Medical School, the University of Chicago, Lagos State University, and the University of Ibadan.
