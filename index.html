QuickLyme®

QuickLyme is a cost-effective, reliable, and rapid method for testing for Lyme bacterium (genus Borrelia) in ticks on the go — without the use of a laboratory. Lyme disease (Borreliosis), which is a growing issue worldwide, is transmitted via ticks and carries dangerous and potentially deadly side effects including rashes, permanent joint damage, swelling, fever, and flu-like symptoms. With our proprietary technology, users are able to quickly determine if they are at risk of contracting Lyme disease, and whether they need to seek further medical attention.
